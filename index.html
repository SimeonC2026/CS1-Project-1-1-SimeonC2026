Pancakes

Description

I like this meal because my mother makes it for me on Sunday mornings. Sunday is known as what my mother calls "Pancake Day."

Ingredients

  • Sweet Rice Flour
  • Sugar
  • Tapioca Starch
  • Ube Puree (Purple Yam Puree)
  • Baking Powder (Sodium Acid Pyrophosphate, Sodium Bicarbonate, Cornstarch, Monocalcium Phosphate)
  • Salt
  • Natural Flavors
  • Vegetable Juice for Color
  • 3/4 cup mix
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Preheat griddle or skillet to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, water, and melted butter or oil.
  3. Add pancake mix and whisk until well combined and batter is smooth.
  4. If batter thickens, add water by teaspoon as needed.
  5. Lightly oil the griddle or skillet.
  6. Use approximately 1/4 cup of batter for each 4-inch pancake and cook for about 2 minutes, or until bubbles start forming and edges are turning brown.
  7. When this happens, flip the pancake and cook for an additional 2 minutes, or until golden brown. Serve immediately when you think that they are ready.
